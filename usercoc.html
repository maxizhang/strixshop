USER CONDUCT

User Conduct & Expectations

The operational standards and behavioral expectations required for all users, customers, and team members within the Strix Shop ecosystem.

SECTION 01

Integrity in Transactions

Orders placed through Strix Shop are considered intentional purchase commitments. False orders, abandoned deliveries, or repeated failure to complete transactions may result in account suspension.

SECTION 02

Prohibition of Hacking

Attempts to exploit vulnerabilities, inject malicious code, bypass authentication systems, access restricted databases, or manipulate digital infrastructure are strictly forbidden.

ZERO TOLERANCE POLICY — Unauthorized access attempts may result in permanent bans and reports to school administration.
SECTION 03

Professional Communication

Communication with staff members and customers must remain respectful and appropriate at all times. Harassment, intimidation, threats, or abusive language will not be tolerated.

SECTION 04

Accurate Information

Users are expected to provide accurate account details, including names, emails, and homeroom information. Impersonation or false identities undermine operational reliability.

SECTION 05

One Account Per Student

Creating multiple accounts for promotional abuse, order manipulation, or ban evasion is considered fraudulent behavior and may lead to account termination.

SECTION 06

Reporting Vulnerabilities

Users who discover glitches, pricing errors, or security weaknesses are expected to report them responsibly instead of exploiting them for personal gain.

SECTION 07

Payment Compliance

Customers must be prepared to complete payment during scheduled delivery windows. Repeated non-payment incidents may result in restricted ordering privileges.

SECTION 08

Respect for School Property

Strix Shop operates within a school environment. All users are expected to comply with school policies and avoid misuse of the platform for prohibited activities.

SECTION 09

Privacy of Others

Users may not access, share, record, or distribute another person's personal information, transaction records, or order history without explicit authorization.

SECTION 10

Enforcement

Violations of this Code may result in account suspension, order cancellation, permanent platform removal, or escalation to school authorities when necessary.
