Strix Shop is committed to honest operations, fair treatment, transparency, and responsible business practices within our school community.

OUR MISSION

WHAT WE PROMISE

Commitments

Transparency

We provide accurate pricing, realistic delivery estimates, and clear communication.

Reliability

Orders are fulfilled carefully and responsibly with the exact products promised.

Accountability

If problems occur, we acknowledge them directly and work toward fair resolutions.

Privacy

Customer data is used strictly for operational purposes and never sold or distributed.

WHAT WE AVOID

Prohibitions

Hidden Charges

Surprise fees and misleading pricing structures are prohibited. There are no shipping fees.

False Advertising

Products must be represented accurately with truthful descriptions and imagery.

Favoritism

Order processing and customer treatment remain equal regardless of personal relationships.

Pressure Tactics
